FAQs

How quickly can AirSync be set up for my business?

Most businesses are fully live within 72 hours. We handle everything — the setup, the configuration, the integrations. You just hop on a 30-minute onboarding call to tell us how your business runs, and we build it from there. Nothing to install, nothing to learn.

Do I need to change my phone number?

Nope. You keep your existing business phone number. Calls get forwarded seamlessly to our AI system. Your customers won't notice any change — except that someone actually picks up every time they call. Same number, smarter answers.

Do I need to learn any new software?

Nope. That's the whole point. AirSync runs in the background connecting to the tools you already use. You don't log into anything new. You don't have to train your team. We manage it, and you see the results — more booked jobs, faster payments, better reviews.

What if I already use ServiceTitan, Jobber, or Housecall Pro?

Even better. AirSync plugs directly into those platforms. Customer data, job history, scheduling — everything stays in sync automatically. No double entry. No switching between apps. It makes the tools you already pay for work even harder.

How much does AirSync cost?

Pricing depends on the size of your operation and which bots you need. Most shops pay less than they were spending on their old combination of answering services, email tools, and CRM subscriptions. Book a call and we'll give you an exact quote — no surprises, no hidden fees.

What happens if the AI can't handle a call?

The AI is trained to handle 90%+ of inbound calls. But when something falls outside its scope — a complex question, an upset customer, or a request for your team specifically — it transfers the call to a real person immediately. You stay in control at all times.
